Gaming For Charity

A Jesuit High School club that hosts video game tournaments for charity

About the club

Jesuit High School's gaming for charity club lets students compete in video game tournaments, the sign up procedes go to American Foundation for Suicide Prevention.

Currently, we are sponsored by Cloud 9, and the tournament winners will receive prizes such as: hoodies, shirts, mousepads, and more.

Currently we are having tournaments for League of Legends, Counter Strike: Global Offensive, Hearthstone, Magic: The Gathering, FIFA, Madden, and Super Smash Brothers, but we are always taking suggestions for new games with an interest.

The Third and First place matches will be Bo3’s All participants must add me; steam name: Tangerine By participating you agree to have read and understood all rules. Absolutely no stream sniping as these matches will be streamed. If there is any poor sportsmanship penalties such as a loss of a ban are possible.
