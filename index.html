Gaming For Charity

A Jesuit High School club that hosts video game tournaments for charity

About the club

Jesuit High School's Gaming for Charity Club lets students compete in video game tournaments in which sign up proceeds go to charities.

We are sponsored by Cloud 9, and the tournament winners will receive prizes such as: hoodies, shirts, mousepads, and more.

We have tournaments for League of Legends, Counter Strike: Global Offensive, Hearthstone, Magic: The Gathering, FIFA, Madden, and Super Smash Brothers, but we are always taking suggestions for new games.
